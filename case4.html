Case 4. Inputs inside a modal dialog

This is the layout where I first hit the problem, in a booking tool I use at work. The date and time inputs sit inside a bottom-sheet dialog, and the dimmed backdrop behind it listens for clicks to close the dialog. In this layout double-tapping the input sometimes does nothing at all, on top of the picker opening by itself when the cursor lands on it.
