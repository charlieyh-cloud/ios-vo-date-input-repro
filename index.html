VoiceOver opens date/time pickers from cursor focus alone

I am a blind VoiceOver user. On iOS Safari, when the VoiceOver cursor lands on a date or time input, the native picker activates on its own, before I double-tap anything. So just swiping through a form keeps opening pickers I never asked for, and I lose my place every time. Moving the cursor onto a field should only announce it; nothing should open until I double-tap, the way text inputs behave.
