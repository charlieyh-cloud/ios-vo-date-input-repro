VoiceOver and Safari's date/time inputs

I am a blind VoiceOver user. On iOS Safari, when the VoiceOver cursor lands on a date or time input, the native picker opens by itself before I double-tap anything. So just swiping through a form keeps opening pickers I never asked for, and I lose my place every time.

I put each test on its own page so it stays easy to navigate:

  • Case 1. Date and time inputs, nothing else
  • Case 2. Same inputs inside a container that has a click listener
  • Case 3. A plain text input, for comparison

To reproduce: turn VoiceOver on, open a case page, and swipe right through the page without double-tapping. On my devices the picker activates as soon as the cursor reaches the input, in both case 1 and case 2. The text input in case 3 behaves normally.
