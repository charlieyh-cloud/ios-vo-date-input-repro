Case 2. Inputs inside a modal dialog

This copies the structure of a booking tool I use at work, where I first hit the problem. When the bottom-sheet dialog opens, the page behind it is marked inert and hidden from assistive tech, the sheet body is a scrollable area, and the page has document-level event listeners the way single-page apps usually do. In that tool, double-tapping the date input often did nothing at all.
